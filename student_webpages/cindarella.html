Cinderella in my own words

It was a dark and rainy night when the lady, Mrs. Roberta, gave birth to a beautiful blond baby girl called Dulce. She chose that name because Dulce means "sweet" in Spanish, and Mrs. Roberta went to Medellin - Colombia when she was 15 and she loved "dulce de leche" a Colombian dessert. But that's another part of the story.

The baby girl was healthy and strong but Mrs. Roberta died that night unfortunately :( leaving the beautiful blond girl alone, but she was a happy and normal girl that talked with rats with pink dresses.

The things Dulce loved the most:

  • Chocolates
  • candies
  • his dad
  • talking to rats and birds

The things Dulce hated the most:

  1. Her stepmother
  2. Her step sisters
  3. to live in the ashes
  4. Not being able to play like a normal girl

She loved reading, this was her favorite poem from Pablo Neruda
